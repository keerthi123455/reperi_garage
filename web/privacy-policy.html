Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Reperi Garage ("we", "our", "us") provides vehicle service booking and related services through our mobile app and website (together, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: your name and email address, used to create and manage your account.
  • Vehicle information: vehicle make, model, and registration number, used to provide accurate service bookings.
  • Location data: your device location, used only when you request Roadside Assistance, to dispatch help to your location.
  • Photos: optionally, photos of your vehicle that you choose to upload (e.g. to our AI Advisor), used to help recommend the right service.
  • Payment information: when you make a payment, it is processed directly by our payment partner, Razorpay. We do not store your card, UPI, or bank details — we only store the transaction status and a reference ID to confirm your booking.
  • Booking and service history: details of services booked, scheduled, and completed through the app.
  • Fleet account data: for business/fleet accounts, company name and associated vehicle and booking records.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and vehicle profile
  • To process and fulfill service bookings and payments
  • To dispatch roadside assistance to your location when requested
  • To provide AI-based service recommendations based on photos or descriptions you submit
  • To communicate booking confirmations, updates, and support responses
  • To improve and maintain the Service

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with the following third parties, solely to operate the Service:

  • Razorpay — to process payments securely
  • Supabase — our backend database and authentication provider, which stores your account, vehicle, and booking data on our behalf

Data Retention

We retain your account and booking information for as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide the Service and comply with legal obligations.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us using the details below. You can also delete your account directly from the app's Profile screen.

Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for use by adults capable of owning or operating a vehicle. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
