Delete Your Reperi Garage Account

Last updated: July 2026

If you'd like to delete your Reperi Garage account and associated data, you have two options:

Option 1: Delete directly in the app

Open the Reperi Garage app, go to Profile → Delete Account, and confirm. This immediately and permanently deletes your profile, vehicles, and booking history.

Option 2: Request deletion without the app

If you no longer have the app installed or can't log in, email us and we'll process your request:

Email keerthishankara2121@gmail.com

Please include the name, email address, or phone number associated with your account so we can locate and remove it.

What gets deleted

  • Your account profile and login credentials
  • Vehicle information linked to your account
  • Booking and service history
  • Fleet account data, if applicable

Some transaction records may be retained where required for legal, tax, or fraud-prevention purposes, even after account deletion. This is limited to what's necessary to meet those obligations.

Processing time

Requests submitted by email are typically processed within 7 business days. You'll receive a confirmation once your data has been deleted.
